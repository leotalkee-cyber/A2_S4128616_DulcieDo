LANYARD

This is a merch design I created for the RMIT Volleyball Club (RVBC), featuring a dragon mascot playing volleyball. The dragon represents passion and the drive to rise high to reach the ball, symbolizing both the team's pursuit of victory and each individual's personal goals. The use of two contrasting colors also allows the wearer of the lanyard to express their own style.
