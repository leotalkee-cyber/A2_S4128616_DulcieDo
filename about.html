ABOUT ME!!

Hi, I'm Dulcie Do, currently studying Digital Media at RMIT. I have a strong passion for creating and for discovering new, unexpected things in everyday life, then translating them into my digital work. I'm quite a dreamy and flexible person, so many of my ideas come from fleeting moments or small details that I happen to notice along the way.

My design style is closely tied to who I am - often leaning towards coquette, vintage, or a certain kind of softness and cuteness. I also really love animals, especially cats. I'd even say I'm a bit of a cat person myself, sometimes calm and quiet, sometimes unpredictable - a little "sunshine and rain" at once.

MY EXPERTISE

2D design is my strongest area and also the one I enjoy the most. I love how something ordinary can be transformed into something special, both in my own eyes and in others', simply through the use of brushes and color.

Sound is, in many ways, the most exciting part of my creative world. It gives me the freedom to design and shape audio exactly the way I imagine, in my own personal way.
