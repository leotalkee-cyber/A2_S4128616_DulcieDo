REIVIENTION

This project explores how Vietnamese traditional values can be modernized through digital image design. The work is inspired by the lion dance, a familiar part of festivals in Vietnam. It aims to encourage people, especially young people, to be confident and stay true to themselves.

The image of the lion dancer trying to remove the mask acts as a metaphor for having the courage to be your authentic self, without being shaped by others' opinions or expectations. For this piece, I used Illustrator to build and color the main design, then brought it into Photoshop to add more details and refine the visuals.
