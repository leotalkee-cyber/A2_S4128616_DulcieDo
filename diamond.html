DIAMOND!

Celebrating beauty and sharing it with others, while spreading a positive message to girls around the world - that is the message "DIAMOND!" aims to convey. Through a sense of romance, lightness, and dreaminess, the project invites viewers into a soft, imaginative atmosphere. At the same time, it reflects my personal design style - someone who is naturally drawn to beauty.
