LINGERING

"LINGERING" is a short film that I shot and edited myself using After Effects. It explores the idea of slowing down and reflecting on the beautiful moments from the past that we may have forgotten in today's fast-paced life. By the time we begin to realize their value, those moments often exist only in our dreams.
